Singapore army and NS movies, with a trailer and where to watch each. Streaming availability changes, so if a link is dead, search the title on [JustWatch](https://www.justwatch.com/sg) for the current source.

## Ah Boys to Men

Jack Neo's comedy franchise about recruits going through BMT on Pulau Tekong. The most well-known NS movies, and the closest thing to a shared cultural reference for enlistees. Start here if you want a feel for [basic military training](/basic-military-training) before you go in.

### Ah Boys to Men (2012)

The original. A spoiled recruit gets his act together through BMT.

<youtube-video id="8AtFZIENdOo"></youtube-video>

Watch on [Netflix](https://www.netflix.com/sg/title/81274442) or [Prime Video](https://www.primevideo.com/detail/Ah-Boys-To-Men/0SAMPWKIC3BTV1X6DCBNKQGRD7).

### Ah Boys to Men 2 (2013)

Picks up in BMT, more of the platoon's antics.

<youtube-video id="kNH6mVBZIJA"></youtube-video>

Watch on [Netflix](https://www.netflix.com/sg/title/81274443).

### Ah Boys to Men 3: Frogmen (2015)

Follows the brutal training of the Naval Diving Unit. A look at one of the tougher vocations.

### Ah Boys to Men 4 (2017)

The recruits return as NSmen for in-camp training.

## Ah Girls Go Army

Jack Neo's female spin-off, told in two parts. Set in a fictional near-future where women are conscripted, it follows the first batch of female recruits through BMT. Same comedy formula as Ah Boys, from the female-recruit angle.

### Ah Girls Go Army (2022)

The recruits report for BMT.

<youtube-video id="qOPvlSIwS3E"></youtube-video>

Watch on [Netflix](https://www.netflix.com/sg/title/81711452).

### Ah Girls Go Army Again (2022)

The sequel, as they push toward their Basic Military Test.

<youtube-video id="ohP0Olo0oZQ"></youtube-video>

Watch on [Netflix](https://www.netflix.com/sg/title/81711454).

## Army Daze (1996)

A classic NS comedy based on Michael Chiang's play. Follows a group of recruits from very different backgrounds thrown together in the same bunk. Older than Ah Boys to Men, but the BMT and bunk-life experience is instantly recognisable.

<youtube-video id="OVtz30pHmKA"></youtube-video>

Free on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DzqTzuJHYPo) (full movie), also on [Netflix](https://www.netflix.com/sg/title/81343400).

## Zombiepura (2018)

Singapore's first widescreen zombie film. A lazy reservist on guard duty has to survive when a virus turns his camp into zombies, teaming up with the commander he can't stand. Plays NS reservist life and camp tropes for laughs.

<youtube-video id="-VSEQFExDC0"></youtube-video>

Free on [Plex](https://watch.plex.tv/movie/zombiepura) and [Tubi](https://tubitv.com/movies/100038834/zombiepura) (region permitting), or rent on [Prime Video](https://www.justwatch.com/sg/movie/zombiepura).

## Every Singaporean Son (documentary)

Not a movie, but the most useful watch here. This is the official documentary series that follows real recruits through [BMT](/basic-military-training) on Pulau Tekong. It is shown to pre-enlistees, so it is the closest you get to seeing actual BMT before you enlist, without the comedy exaggeration. Later seasons cover [SCS](/specialist-cadet-school) and other stages.

<youtube-video id="gxmp8nwQ43Q"></youtube-video>

Free on YouTube. Watch the [full series playlist](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=every+singaporean+son+full+episodes) (search for the season you want).
