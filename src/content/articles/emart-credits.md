eMart credits are a yearly allowance for SAF kit. They are separate from your cash allowance. You cannot withdraw them or turn them into money. You can only spend them at eMart, on personal and expendable items like admin tees, socks, towels, and running shoes.

Most people forget they have them, then rush to use them before they expire. Don't do that. They are basically free gear.

## How many credits you get

Credits depend on your service (Army, Air Force, Navy), vocation, and status. They are pro-rated by your enlistment and ORD dates.

### Army

Status | Credits
-- | --
Active combat vocation | 260 per year
Active non-combat vocation | 170 per year
Permanent PES E NSF | 120 per year
NSman | 113 every 2 years (226 every 4 years)
SAFVC volunteer | 50 per completed year of service

### Air Force (RSAF)

Status | Credits
-- | --
Active combat, Group A | 270 per year
Active combat, Group B | 310 per year
Active non-combat, Group A | 250 per year
Active non-combat, Group B | 350 per year
Permanent PES E NSF | 130 per year
NSman | 115 every 2 years (230 every 4 years)
SAFVC volunteer | 50 per completed year of service

### Navy (RSN)

Status | Credits
-- | --
Group A | 250 per year
Group B | 275 per year
Group C | 340 per year
Group D | 375 per year
NSman | 115 every 2 years (230 every 4 years)
SAFVC volunteer | 50 per completed year of service

Check your exact balance and allocation in the OneNS app or portal.

## Expiry and rollover

Credits do not last forever.

- Unused credits can be carried forward **once**, to the next top-up cycle.
- After that, they expire and are gone.
- So spend older credits first.

You need to be an active NSF, Regular, or NSman to use eMart credits. Allocation is pro-rated by your enlistment and ORD dates. Check your balance and expiry in OneNS.

## Is it covered by credits?

eMart items are colour-labelled, so you know how you can pay before you reach the counter.

Label | What it means
-- | --
Red | Credits only
White | Credits or cash
Yellow | Cash only

Some items are credits only (Army No. 4 uniforms, for example), some take either, and some are cash only. Check the label before you assume.

## Where you can use them

Credits work at:

- In-camp eMarts
- The commercial Lifestylemart outlets (SAFRA Punggol, The Chevrons, CMPB)
- The OneNS online eMart

Credits do **not** work at:

- Outside army shops like Black Tactical, the Beach Road army market, or G Military. Those are cash only.
- The Lifestylemart store on Lazada. It sells selected SAF items to the public, but only takes normal Lazada payment, not credits.

See the full list of outlets and addresses on the [eMarts and army shops](/army-shop-emart) page.

## What to buy

Best value is anything you would otherwise pay real money for.

Prices change. The figures below are recent public Lifestylemart and Lazada spot-checks, not your exact OneNS credit price, but they show the ballpark.

Item | Approx price | Why
-- | -- | --
Running shoes (New Balance / ASICS) | $42 to $50 | Biggest bang for your credits
Admin / round neck tee | $5 to $6 | Light, comfortable, wear it anywhere
Black socks | $2 to $3 a pair | Stock up, never buy socks again
Microfibre towel | ~$5 | Gym, travel, swimming
Torchlight | ~$7 | Better than your phone torch
No. 3 black shoes | low | Works for office and formal wear
Multitool | low | Bottle opener, screwdriver, cutter
Insect repellent | low | Useful well beyond camp
Ear plugs | low | Concerts, flights, light sleepers
Kiwi polish and brush | low | If you keep wearing leather shoes

Quick rule: spend on consumables you will keep using (socks, repellent, polish) and on the expensive stuff (shoes, towel). Skip anything you will never touch in civilian life just to burn credits.

## How to order

You have three ways.

### Walk-in

- **Commercial Lifestylemart outlets** (SAFRA Punggol, The Chevrons, CMPB): longer hours, weekends included.
- **In-camp eMarts**: usually Mon to Fri office hours, and you need camp access.

### OneNS online eMart

Order in the OneNS app or portal, then pick self-collection or home delivery.

- Delivery or collection takes 3 to 5 working days from your confirmation email.
- Home delivery costs $12.48 per order, payable with credits or a credit card.

### Lifestylemart on Lazada

Selected SAF items, open to the public, 24/7. Next-day delivery, excluding weekends and public holidays.

Note: this channel does **not** accept eMart credits. You pay with normal Lazada payment methods.

## Tips

- Check your balance and expiry in OneNS early, not the week before they expire.
- Spend older credits first.
- Prioritise shoes and towels. They cost the most outside.
- Take care of your issued kit so you are not wasting credits replacing lost items. See [saving money in NS](/saving-money).
- Hours and stock change, so call ahead before going down to a specific outlet.
