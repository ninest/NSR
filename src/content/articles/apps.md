A running list of apps, websites, and bots that actually help during NS. Official tools first, then fitness, perks, and gear.

## Official

### OneNS

The official MINDEF app and web portal. It replaced the old NS Portal, so this is where most admin now happens.

What you can do:

- Check in fast with QR code or geolocation for ICT and mobilisation
- Book and reschedule [IPPT](/ippt)
- Apply for deferment and check excuse status
- Track milestones like awards, credits, and disbursements
- View ICT training records

Get it: [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=sg.mindef.ns.onens) or [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/sg/app/onens/id1619937291). Web version at [ns.gov.sg](https://www.ns.gov.sg/).

### Singpass

You need Singpass to log in to OneNS and most government services. Set up the app early so you are not stuck at a login screen on enlistment day.

Get it: [singpass.gov.sg](https://www.singpass.gov.sg/).

### LifeSG

This is where your NS LifeSG credits land. Use it to receive and spend them, and to redeem other government payouts.

Pre-enlistees and parents also get NS pre-enlistment guides and a milestone tracker in the app.

Get it: [supportgowhere.life.gov.sg/lifesg](https://supportgowhere.life.gov.sg/lifesg) or search "LifeSG" in your app store.

## Fitness and IPPT

### IPPT Plus

A brand new IPPT app with enhanced experience for all users. This can be used by regulars, NSF, NSmen, regardless of gender, exemptions and elite force or not.

Download via: [ippt-plus.com](https://ippt-plus.com/)

<youtube-video id="is5PuWIPTbc"></youtube-video>

### IPPT score calculator

A quick web tool to work out your score and award from your push-ups, sit-ups and 2.4km time. No download needed, just enter your numbers.

Try it at: [ipptscore.com](https://ipptscore.com/)

### Running apps for the 2.4km

The 2.4km run is worth the most points on the [IPPT](/ippt), so it is the easiest place to gain. A run tracker with GPS pace and interval workouts helps a lot.

- [Nike Run Club](https://www.nike.com/sg/nrc-app): free guided runs and pace tracking
- [Strava](https://www.strava.com/): track every run and watch your pace trend over weeks

Any tracker works. The point is to see your pace, not run blind.

## Perks and savings

### SAFRA

NSFs get a complimentary trial membership. Worth setting up for the deals alone.

What you get:

- Discounts at 3,000+ merchant outlets island-wide
- eVouchers in the app
- Access to clubhouse facilities and gyms
- An Appreciation Package near [ORD](/ord-countdown)

Get it: [safra.sg](https://www.safra.sg/membership/highlights-for-nsfs) and the SAFRA app.

See also: [saving money in NS](/saving-money).

## Gear

### eMart Online

Order SAF gear and replace worn-out kit with your [eMart credits](/emart-credits) without queueing at the shop. Pick self-collection or home delivery.

Pay with eMart credits, Visa, Mastercard, or PayLah!.

More: [army shops and eMarts](/army-shop-emart), and [what eMart credits are](/emart-credits).
