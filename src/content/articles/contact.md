## Safety hotline (9-SAFETY-1)
9723 3891

Call this to report any safety incident or lapse during NS training ([official guide](https://www.life.gov.sg/guides/parent/safety-during-ns)).

## CMPB
- Hotline: 1800-367-6767 (1800-eNSNSNS)
- Hotline international: +65 6567-6767
- Operating hours: Mon to Fri, 8am to 5.30pm SGT (excluding public holidays)
- Email: [contact@ns.gov.sg](mailto:contact@ns.gov.sg)

## Counselling helplines
For servicemen or family who are worried about how someone is coping. If you are a parent, call a counsellor for advice rather than your son's officer.

- SAF: 1800-278-0022
- SCDF: 1800-286-6666
- SPF: 1800-255-1151

## Reaching your son in camp
His unit contact number is on his Enlistment Notice. Ask him to share it with you once he is in. If you do not have it, call the CMPB hotline above. For a true emergency, go through the unit or these hotlines rather than waiting for him to reply.
