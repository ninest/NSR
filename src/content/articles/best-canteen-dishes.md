- Selarang Camp – Nasi Lemak

- Sembawang Camp – 50¢ Prata
- Nee Soon Camp – Wantan Mee
- Changi Naval Base – Laksa
- Ayer Rajah Camp – Malay Fried Chicken Wing
- Sungei Gedong Camp – Black Pepper Chicken Chop
